Our Mission

Serve the community. Keep streets clean. Support veterans and first responders.

Why we started

CurbEase Bin Valet was started to remove a small but annoying pain from busy families—getting bins to the curb and back—while showing up as good neighbors. We’re law‑enforcement owned and operated in North Texas, and we bring that same focus on reliability, respect, and safety to every home we visit.

Community first

Punctual service, quiet early‑morning routes, and neighborhood‑friendly conduct. We treat every driveway like our own and keep streets tidy on pickup day.

  • Background‑checked staff
  • Secure handling of gate codes & notes
  • Text reminders & clear communication

Clean & safe streets

Consistent set‑out and return reduces missed pickups and keeps bins from blocking sidewalks or blowing into streets. Our team follows clear standards to protect property and improve curb appeal.

Giving back

A portion of every subscription supports Wounded Warrior Project and Tunnel to Towers.
