About
CurbEase Bin Valet was founded to make life easier for our neighbors while keeping our community looking its best. As a law‑enforcement–owned and family‑run business, we value trust, dependability, and service. Every pickup is done with professionalism and care, because small details make a big difference in the pride we share for our city.

We’re also committed to giving back. A portion of our efforts supports the Wounded Warrior Project, honoring those who have sacrificed for our freedom. Serving our community means more than curbside—it’s about contributing to a cleaner, safer, and stronger place for everyone.
